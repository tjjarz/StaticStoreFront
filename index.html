Cube of Space Filling

This lovely cube does exactly what it's name implies, it takes up space!

Deploy it anywhere where you might need some space consumed!

It also makes a decent seat in a pinch though that will of course void your warranty.

First released in 1985, it is far and away the favorite space-filling cube in all known galactic regions, handly beating out all the competition for space consumption. The uncharitable might suggest this is due solely to it's being the ONLY such cube, but anyone who would say that is clearly just jealous of it's success in the market.

Features the words "Don't Panic" in large, friendly letters on reverse side.
